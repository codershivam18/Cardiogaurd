⚠️ Cardiac Arrest Warning Signs

Recognizing early symptoms significantly improves survival chances.

⚠️
Sudden Collapse

Unexpected loss of consciousness without prior warning signs.

💔
No Pulse or Breathing

Absence of detectable heartbeat and stopped breathing.

😵
Unresponsiveness

No reaction to touch, voice, or external stimulation.

🌬️
Severe Shortness of Breath

Sudden difficulty breathing or gasping for air.

🔥
Chest Pain or Pressure

Intense discomfort, tightness, or pressure in the chest.

🚨 Emergency Notice: If someone shows signs of cardiac arrest, seek immediate medical assistance.
